# Product Brochure — Compliance Rubric

## Required Sections
1. **Headline / Tagline** — Compelling headline that communicates value proposition
2. **Product Overview** — Clear, concise description of what the product does (2-3 sentences)
3. **Key Benefits** — Customer-facing benefits listed (not just features)
4. **Feature Highlights** — 3–6 standout features described with brief explanations
5. **Target Audience** — Ideal customer profile or use cases identified
6. **Social Proof / Credibility** — Client references, statistics, or awards referenced
7. **Call to Action** — Clear next step for the reader (contact, demo, trial)
8. **Contact Details** — Company contact information included

## Quality Criteria
9. **Benefit-Led Language** — Copy focuses on customer outcomes, not technical specifications
10. **Brand Voice** — Tone matches brand guide (professional, confident, clear)
11. **No Jargon** — Technical terms explained or avoided for non-specialist audience
12. **Scannable Format** — Uses bullet points, subheadings, and short paragraphs
13. **Consistency** — Product name, capitalisation, and terminology consistent throughout
