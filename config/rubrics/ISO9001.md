# ISO 9001:2015 — Document Quality Rubric

## Documentation Requirements (Clause 7.5)
1. **Document Identification** — Title, version number, date, and owner clearly stated
2. **Purpose and Scope** — Document purpose and applicability explicitly defined
3. **Controlled Terminology** — Technical terms and abbreviations defined or referenced
4. **Approval Status** — Document status (draft / under review / approved) indicated
5. **Version Control** — Revision history or change log present

## Process Documentation (Clause 4.4)
6. **Process Inputs** — Inputs or triggers for the process clearly identified
7. **Process Outputs** — Expected outputs or deliverables specified
8. **Process Owner** — Responsible party identified
9. **Measurement Criteria** — Success metrics or KPIs defined

## Customer Requirements (Clause 8)
10. **Requirements Completeness** — All customer requirements captured
11. **Acceptance Criteria** — Clear sign-off criteria stated
12. **Risk Identification** — Key risks and mitigations documented

## Continual Improvement (Clause 10)
13. **Lessons Learned / Review Notes** — Provision for review observations or improvement notes
14. **Non-Conformance Handling** — Process for handling deviations or exceptions described
