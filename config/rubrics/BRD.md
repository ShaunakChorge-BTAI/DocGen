# Business Requirements Document (BRD) — Compliance Rubric

## Required Sections
1. **Executive Summary** — High-level overview of the business problem and proposed solution
2. **Business Objectives** — Specific, measurable goals the project must achieve
3. **Scope** — Clear boundary of what is and is not included; explicit out-of-scope items listed
4. **Stakeholders** — Key stakeholders with their roles and interests identified
5. **Problem Statement / Current State** — Existing situation and pain points described
6. **Proposed Solution** — Recommended approach and justification provided
7. **Functional Requirements** — Detailed, numbered business requirements present
8. **Non-Functional Requirements** — Performance, security, and compliance requirements included
9. **Assumptions and Constraints** — Known limitations and assumed conditions listed
10. **Timeline and Milestones** — High-level project timeline or phases indicated
11. **Budget / Cost Considerations** — Budget context or estimate referenced

## Quality Criteria
12. **Requirement Traceability** — Each requirement links to a business objective
13. **SMART Requirements** — Requirements are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
14. **No Placeholder Text** — No "TBD", "TODO", or "to be confirmed" in final sections
15. **Version Information** — Document version and date present
